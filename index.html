PeRo

Born in the vibrant cultural tapestry of Nigeria, Peter Fabian popularly known as Pero is a dynamic and soulful Afrobeat artist and songwriter, captivating audiences with a unique blend of rhythmic storytelling and infectious melodies. The German based artist hailing from Imo State, Pero discovered a passion for music at an early age, inspired by the rich musical heritage of Nigeria. Growing up in this diverse environment laid the foundation for his eclectic musical style. As Pero continues to evolve as an artist, he is driven by a commitment to push the boundaries of Afrobeat and contribute to the global appreciation of African music.
